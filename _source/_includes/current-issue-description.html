Current Issue: Volume 17

With this release of the Journal of Southern Religion, the journal begins the process of rolling releases. Volume 17 features an article on segregationists' hermeneutical approaches to scripture in the 1960s and an article on the J.R. Graves's newspaper accumen and the expansion of his particular form of Baptists beliefs. Eleven book reviews of recent books in the field round out Volume 17 of the JSR.

About the JSR

Welcome to the first scholarly journal devoted to the study of religion in the American South. The Journal of Southern Religion is a fully peer-reviewed academic journal reflecting the best traditions of objective and critical scholarship. It is an open-access publication, published free of cost in its entirety on the Internet.
